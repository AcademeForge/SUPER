Frequently Asked Questions

Have Questions?

Are scholarships available?

+
Merit-based and need-based scholarships are available.

What is AcademeForge?

+
AcademeForge is a student-focused learning platform built around practical skills, creativity, and real-world learning beyond traditional classrooms.

Are admissions open right now?

+
No. Admissions in 2026 are currently paused while we rebuild and improve the platform, programs, and learning experience.

When will admissions start again?

+
Admissions are expected to open in upcoming academic sessions. Updates will be shared officially through the website and social platforms.

Is this beginner friendly?

+
Yes. Programs are being designed for both complete beginners and students who already have some basic knowledge.

What kind of skills will students learn?

+
Students will work on practical skills like digital tools, communication, creative thinking, technology basics, problem-solving, and project-based learning.

Will there be live classes or recorded content?

+
Learning may include a mix of live sessions, recorded lessons, workshops, and practical activities for flexible learning.

Do I need an expensive laptop or setup?

+
No. A normal laptop or mobile device with a stable internet connection is enough for most beginner-level learning activities.

Will students get mentorship and support?

+
Yes. Students will be able to interact with mentors, ask doubts, and receive guidance throughout the learning journey.

Will certificates be provided?

+
Yes. Certificates may be provided for selected programs, activities, or learning tracks after successful completion.

What makes AcademeForge different?

+
AcademeForge focuses more on practical learning, consistency, execution, and real growth instead of only theory, memorization, or marks.
